About Arun

Arun Pant is a certified yoga teacher and wellness guide from Dehradun, Uttarakhand. On a mission to get the tools of yoga into homes and hearts, Arun creates accessible practices that inspire people of all ages, shapes, and sizes.

With a unique blend of traditional Hatha yoga, modern mindfulness, and breathwork, Arun's approach focuses on finding what feels good for your body and mind. His classes are designed to help you reconnect with yourself, reduce stress, and build strength—both physical and mental.
