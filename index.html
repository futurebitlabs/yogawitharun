About Arun

A grounded teacher with a calm, practical approach to yoga.

Arun Pant is a yoga teacher and wellness guide from Uttarakhand who focuses on simple, sustainable practice. His classes draw from Hatha yoga, breath awareness, stillness, and mindful strength so students can feel both supported and challenged.

Whether you are completely new to yoga or returning after a long break, Arun teaches in a way that helps you build trust in your body, breathe more fully, and move with intention instead of pressure.

What Practice Feels Like

A calm experience designed around steadiness, not performance.

The goal is not to force advanced shapes. It is to build a relationship with your breath, your posture, and your attention. Each session balances effort with ease so the practice feels sustainable and meaningful.

1. Arrive and settle

Grounding breathwork and simple mobility to release stiffness.

2. Move with awareness

Intentional sequences that support strength, flexibility, and focus.

3. Leave lighter

Closing rest and breath practices that bring clarity back into the day.
